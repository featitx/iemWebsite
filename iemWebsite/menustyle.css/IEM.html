ABOUT US

Ideal Educational Movement


Ideal Educational Movement (IEM) is a registered charitable organization established in 1993 with the main objective to educate the students of Urdu medium schools in their schools curriculum, Maharashtra and National Talent Search, Government Scholarship Examinations and various educational upliftment activities, so that the students can achieve better percentage which shall enable them build a career of their choice.




DR. Mohammed Ali Patankar

(President, IEM.)
